What Are Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs)?

Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs) are privately pooled investment vehicles that collect funds from investors to invest in diverse assets beyond traditional stocks and bonds.

In India, AIFs are regulated by SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India) and are primarily targeted at high-net-worth individuals (HNIs) and institutional investors.

Why Choose (AIFs)?

Diversification:

Reduce market volatility by investing in non-traditional assets.

Professional Management:

Expert fund managers handle investments for optimized growth.
